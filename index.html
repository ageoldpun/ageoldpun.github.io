    • Speech and Language Connections provides individual evaluations and therapy for children ages 3-18 who have delays in speech and/or language. A child with a speech delay may have difficulty with producing speech sounds that makes their speech unintelligible to his family or his teacher. A child who has a language delay may have difficulty composing a grammatically correct sentence, comprehending verbal information, or forming complete sentences to share his or her ideas. Or if your child doesn’t use words, but needs a system for communicating his/her basic needs, then we provide individualized therapy for your child to create that system (i.e., using pictures, sign language, a computerized program that has voice output). The mission of our practice is to help children connect with their environment and the people in their lives in a meaningful way.

    • Additionally, Speech and Language Connections offers:
    • Afternoon and evening appointments.
    • In-home therapy sessions.
    • Free onsite parking.
    • Homework activities to support the child’s continued growth.
