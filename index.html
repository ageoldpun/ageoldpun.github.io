Speech and Language Connections provides individual evaluations and therapy for children ages 3-18 who have delays in speech and/or language.

Our mission is to help each child connect with the people in his/her environment in a meaningful way through communication.

Communication: the act or process of using words, sounds, signs, or behaviors to express or exchange information or to express your ideas, thoughts, feelings, etc., to someone else (http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/communication)
